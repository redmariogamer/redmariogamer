European Voyage: Legends of the Hidden Relics (

A solo game to learn about life around Europe that gives Assassin Creed's vibes.

A co-op 1–4 player adventure set in a 3D European-inspired world. Players are guided by a mysterious professor who advises them to go on a race to recover ancient relics before an evil sorcerer erases the relics and the memory itself. Exploration, decision-making, and discovery are the neccessary factors to drive the quest.

Candy Doom

The game focuses on players who are turned from humans into gingerbread men in a candy world. Your goal is to explore the candy lands and defeat any enemies that get in your way so you can relax by your campfire. You start with just your trusty chomper and water gun to fight off swarms of candy monsters. As you move through each level, you’ll find new weapons and tools that help you better tackle future challenges. Once you finish the game, you get to play a new version called “new game plus,” which allows you to use all the tools you’ve earned to explore even more areas of the map that were previously locked.
